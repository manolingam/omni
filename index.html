😀 Who we are 😀

We are a distributed remote team building a platform around ethereum to serve a central location for most of your digital activities pinned to your own wallet address.

🖌 What we are doing 🖌

We work towards Web 3.0 and have so far tested and building,

  • Token Distributor - A place where you can share and distribute tokens via email.
  • User pages - Your own custom page that can be shared with friends.
  • Whiteborad - Jot down your thoughts.
  • Twitter People - Follow and manage users from twitter.
  • Virtual Farm - Fun way to build and maintain your own virtual world.
  • Music - Listen with your tokens.
  • IPFS Images - Store your images in a decentralized way.
